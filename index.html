ABOUT LUNAVELL LTD

DESIGNING BEAUTY. CRAFTING CALMNESS.

Lunavell LTD is a London-based company designing beauty, crafting calmness and delivering long-term emotional value. We create sensorial experiences that make everyday life softer — inviting clarity, warmth and gentle presence into modern city routines.

MISSION

A QUIET COMMITMENT TO EASING THE DAY.

Lunavell’s mission is to quietly upgrade the emotional climate of the spaces we live in — from small London flats to shared homes and work corners that double as refuge. We believe that light, texture, temperature and tiny rituals can completely shift how a day feels.

Instead of chasing loud wellness promises, we design calm, considered solutions that simply make it easier to breathe: discreet objects, light-driven experiences and tactile details that soften overstimulation.

Every Lunavell decision is guided by three questions:

  • Does this create more calm than noise?
  • Will it still feel relevant and beautiful in five winters?
  • Does it respect the pace and reality of urban life?
APPROACH

MINIMAL OBJECTS. MAXIMUM CALM.

Our design approach is intentionally restrained: neutral palettes, soft silhouettes and quiet, cloud-inspired details. We work where beauty, practicality and emotional comfort meet — always prioritising long-term use over momentary trends.

Lunavell focuses on objects and experiences that:

  • Support daily routines in darker, colder months.
  • Offer sensory comfort without visual clutter.
  • Feel timeless in both minimal and layered interiors.

We collaborate with carefully selected partners and manufacturers, ensuring that every future Lunavell product aligns with our standards of calm aesthetics, safety and durability.
