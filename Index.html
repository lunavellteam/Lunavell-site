About Lunavell LTD

Lunavell LTD is a private company registered in the United Kingdom. We operate in retail and wholesale of wellness, beauty-adjacent and home lifestyle products, with an emphasis on seasonal comfort and calm technology.

Approach

Our work starts with how people actually live: small flats, long commutes, grey skies and busy minds. We develop minimalist, durable products that combine a clean visual language with functional, everyday benefits. Think: sunrise lamps for darker seasons, discreet dryers for wet winters, and comfort devices that feel at home in a calm, neutral interior.

Privacy & data

Lunavell LTD respects customer privacy. We only collect information that is necessary to process orders, operate our business and comply with legal requirements. Data is not sold to third parties. A full, detailed privacy policy will be published before any direct-to-consumer sales begin.

Returns & refunds

We follow fair and transparent returns rules. Defective or damaged products can be returned or refunded in line with UK consumer law and the policies of each marketplace where our products are sold (for example, Amazon’s own returns policy when selling via FBA). Product-specific terms will be clearly stated on each listing.

Shipping & delivery

Shipping methods and delivery times depend on the channel (such as Amazon FBA, wholesale or other partners). We work with established logistics providers to ensure safe, traceable delivery and will always display estimated delivery times at checkout or on product listings.

Terms & conditions

All sales made by Lunavell LTD are subject to our terms and conditions, which will be published in full in a dedicated document. These include information about pricing, orders, warranties, limitation of liability and governing law (England & Wales).
